Congratulations, you have the MyCHIPs mobile app installed!

But it looks like you are not yet connected to a CHIP service provider.

The GotChoices Foundation runs a provider here or if you are talented with computers you may be able to be your own service provider.

Your provider can issue you a ticket link (or QR code) that will launch the app and get you connected.
